AI Diet & Nutrition Planner

Personalized Health, Powered by AI, Guided by Experts

The Challenge

  • One-Size-Fits-All Fails: Generic diet plans don't account for individual medical conditions or goals.
  • Lack of Accountability: Users struggle to track their progress consistently.
  • Disconnect with Experts: Traditional apps replace dietitians instead of connecting users with them.

Our Solution

  • Smart AI Generation: Google Gemini generates personalized diet & workout plans instantly.
  • Expert Customization: Verified Nutritionists can review and edit AI-generated plans for medical safety.
  • Integrated Ecosystem: A single platform for Users, Nutritionists, and Admins.

Nutritionist Portal

  • Interactive Diet Plan Editor
  • Real-time Progress Monitoring (Weight & Water)
  • End-to-End Encrypted Chat

Key Features for Users

  • Comprehensive Assessment (BMI, BMR calculations)
  • AI Meal & Recipe Engine
  • Daily Tracking Logs
  • Direct Messaging with Nutritionists
